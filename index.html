Innovation Hackathon 2023

July 15-17, 2023
Innovation Hub Campus
500+ Participants

About The Event

Join us for our annual Innovation Hackathon where students, developers, designers, and entrepreneurs come together to create innovative solutions to real-world problems. This 48-hour event will feature workshops, mentorship from industry experts, and amazing prizes for the winning teams.

The Innovation Hackathon is a platform for creative minds to collaborate, innovate, and build solutions that can make a difference. Whether you're a seasoned developer or just starting your journey, this event offers something for everyone.

Who Can Participate?

The Innovation Hackathon is open to:

  • Students from colleges and universities
  • Professional developers and designers
  • Entrepreneurs and startup founders
  • Anyone passionate about technology and innovation
