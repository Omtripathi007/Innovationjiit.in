About Us

Pioneering Innovation at JIIT

Our Story

The Innovation Hub at JIIT is a dynamic ecosystem that fosters creativity, entrepreneurship, and technological advancement. Established with a vision to transform ideas into reality, we provide a platform for students, faculty, and industry experts to collaborate and innovate.

Our mission is to nurture the next generation of innovators and entrepreneurs by providing them with the resources, mentorship, and opportunities they need to succeed in today's rapidly evolving technological landscape.

Flagship Events

Ride Hack

Ride Hack is our annual mobility-focused hackathon that brings together innovators to solve transportation challenges. Participants work in teams to develop creative solutions for urban mobility, sustainable transportation, and smart city integration.

300+ Participants
36 Hours
₹1 Lakh in Prizes

Innovation Hackathon

Our flagship Innovation Hackathon is a 48-hour marathon of coding, designing, and problem-solving. Teams tackle real-world challenges across various domains including AI, blockchain, IoT, and sustainability, with mentorship from industry experts.

500+ Participants
48 Hours
₹2 Lakhs in Prizes

Startup Pitch Competition

Our Startup Pitch Competition provides a platform for early-stage entrepreneurs to present their innovative ideas to a panel of investors and industry experts. Winners receive seed funding, mentorship, and incubation support to turn their ideas into successful ventures.

100+ Startups
20+ Investors
₹5 Lakhs Investment

Our Vision

We envision a future where innovation thrives at the intersection of technology and entrepreneurship. Our goal is to create a vibrant ecosystem that nurtures creativity, fosters collaboration, and empowers individuals to transform their ideas into impactful solutions.

Through our various programs and initiatives, we aim to build a community of innovators who are not just problem-solvers but also visionaries shaping the future of technology and business.
