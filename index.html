About Innovation Hub

Pioneering the future of technology and entrepreneurship

Who We Are

The JIIT Innovation Hub is a space where creativity meets technology. We empower students and innovators with labs, mentorship, and resources to turn ideas into real-world solutions, fostering a strong culture of research and entrepreneurship.

Our Flagship Activities

Ride Hack

Ride Hack is the flagship hackathon of the JIIT Innovation Hub, designed to bring together creative minds to solve real-world problems through technology and innovation. It provides a platform for students to showcase their skills, build prototypes, and pitch ideas, while receiving mentorship from experts and exposure to industry.
