What We Do

At the Innovation Hub, JIIT Noida, we nurture ideas and transform them into impactful solutions through mentorship, modern labs, and startup support. We host flagship events like RIDE-Hack, Startup Weekend, and the E-Summit, along with regular workshops, innovation challenges, and expert sessions. RIDE Hack'25

48-Hour Innovation Challenge

NOV 1 2025

About RIDE Hack'25

RIDE Hack'25 is the flagship hackathon of the JIIT Innovation, designed to bring together creative minds to solve real-world problems through technology and innovation. This 48-hour intensive coding competition will challenge students, developers, designers, and entrepreneurs to create innovative solutions under the theme of "Smart Mobility and Sustainable Transportation".

Prizes & Rewards

1st Place
₹1 Lakh
Cash prize + Mentorship opportunity
2nd Place
₹50 Thousand Each
Cash prize + Incubation support (Distributed among two)
3rd Place
₹25 Thousand Each
Cash prize + Workshop vouchers (Distributed among two)

Register Now

Registration is open to all students and professionals. Teams can consist of 2-4 members.
